- Auto-detects file changes every 30 seconds and refreshes automatically
- Progressive loading — search available immediately while additional data still loads in the background
- Runs in the system tray — always ready without keeping a window open
- Export search results to Excel or CSV with one click

**Excel-to-Database Integration (Unique):**
- A purpose-built data integration layer connects Excel workbooks directly to the local analytical database engine
- Supports batch insertion of 2,500 records at a time and multi-table SQL queries — reducing crashes and manual handling on high-volume files
- When a sales sheet is prepared (e.g. up to 1,000 devices across 10 daily bills), the VBA macro queries the database for every device automatically
- Confirms each device exists in inventory and that the sale price matches the purchase record
- Flags price mismatches in red — directly inside the sales sheet before finalising
- This is the post-payment confirmation step — verifying delivery matches what was paid

**How It Was Built (AI-assisted):**

## DETAIL PAGE — Mobile Hub (Full Experience)

**Title:** MIS Executive — Mobile Hub
**Subtitle:** Oct 2022 – Present · Udyog Nagar, New Delhi · 3+ years · Sole data and automation person on a 4-person team

**Business Context:**
- Mobile Hub purchases second-hand mobile phones in bulk lots from liquidation platforms. Each lot contains between 93 and 1,000+ phones, condition-graded from best (S1) to lowest (S5).
- Brands include Apple, Samsung, Google, OnePlus, Xiaomi, Motorola, and others. Monthly stock value ranges from ₹5 to ₹20 crore.
- Stock is stored across 2 warehouses — Noida and Bilaspur. Every device is tracked by warehouse location in the master database.
- Every purchase involves supplier pricing files, order confirmation, payment, post-payment file receipt, delivery verification, and inventory recording — all through systems I built.
- I am the only person handling data and automation on the team. A separate accountant manages Tally and rechecks financial records independently.

**Full Daily Workflow — 8 Steps:**

1. **Receive Supplier Lot File** — A pricing reference file arrives for each purchase lot — contains product codes, brand, model, condition grade, base price, agreed pricing structure (flat rates, percentage discounts, or adjusted margins), and the final agreed rate per unit.

2. **Gmail Auto-Download (50–100 Order Files)** — The Gmail automation script downloads all supplier order file attachments for the day — each file contains product codes, product title, brand, grade, base price, and final value. Sorted by file type and warehouse automatically. Duplicates skipped.

3. **Rate Calculation & Power Query Processing** — Agreed pricing formulas (accommodating flat rates, percentage discounts, or adjusted margins) are applied to calculate the final net purchase rate per unit. Power Query then merges all order files, cleans the data, and produces a master workbook: one sheet per lot, a merged data sheet, and a summary showing total quantity, total amount, condition breakdown, and brand split. Example: one run covers 11 lots and 1,021 phones.

4. **Payment Released** — Payment is confirmed and made to the supplier based on the agreed order rate and total. This happens before receiving the detailed invoice and stock files — the supplier sends those after payment is received on their end.

5. **Receive Invoice and Stock Detail Files** — After payment is received by the supplier, they send invoice files (product code, price, tax breakdown, total per unit) and stock detail files (warehouse serial number, IMEI, brand, model, condition grade). These are auto-downloaded via the same Gmail script.

6. **3-Way Stock Reconciliation** — Compare three data sources: what was originally ordered, what actually arrived, and what was charged. Short-delivered units, condition grade changes, and pricing differences all identified. Any discrepancy is documented and escalated for correction against the payment already made.

7. **Update Master Yearly Database** — Every device from the batch is entered into the master database — IMEI, warehouse serial number, condition grade, purchase amount, warehouse location (Noida or Bilaspur), and batch reference. Single source of truth for all inventory across the business.

8. **Post-Payment Delivery Confirmation** — A final check comparing the payment made against the confirmed delivery and invoice data. The Excel-to-database integration queries every device in the sales batch, confirms each unit is recorded correctly in inventory, and flags any discrepancy between what was paid and what was received. Tracked and resolved if any difference is found.

**Reporting Deliverables:**
- Daily stock summaries — quantity per batch, condition breakdown, brand split, total amount
- Weekly, 15-day, and monthly batch performance reports covering sales volume and stock movement
- Customer sales sheets listing every device sold: condition, amount, serial number, IMEI — auto-generated per sale
- Batch payments, open balances, and customer receipts tracked for up to 1,000 devices across 10 daily sales bills
- Management pivot tables: stock by condition, brand, warehouse, seller

**Other Key Contributions:**
- Wrote full business process documentation covering the entire data workflow — used as operational reference and context for AI-assisted tool development
- Reduced stock selection time by 1 hour using Excel pivot tables, slicers, and cross-referenced formulas
- Built scan sheets and sales invoices that auto-populate from inventory — zero manual entry at sale time
- Caught multiple post-payment delivery discrepancies over time — traced each back to source, escalated, and got corrections confirmed against payment records
- Created SOPs covering reconciliation logic, file naming rules, workflow steps, and data standards
